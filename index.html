About Us

Hydro Diamond Pressure Washing is pressure washing business located in Northwest Arkansas. We provide pressure washing services in Bentonville, Bella Vista, Rogers, Springsdale, Centerton, Lowelland the surrounding areas. Loyalty and Quality is what we strive for! Contact us at (479) 427-2095 or hydrodiamondpw@gmail.com for your Pressure Washing Needs!

Our Top Services: We specialize in House Wash, Fence/ Deck Cleaning, Concrete Cleaning, and Gutter Shining. If you don't see a local pressure washing service listed please give us a call to learn if we can help.

Our Team: Hydro Diamond is owned and operated by Tristin Lee. Our team has the expertise and equipment needed for all your home washing and commercial washing needs. Tristin and the team is located in Northwest Arkansas and proud to be a local pressure washing business serving the community.

Our Guarantee: We take pride in providing the highest quality pressure washing services for the local community. We treat customers like family as we know how meaningful is can be to have a clean driveway, sidewalk, patio, home, store front, office space and more.
